How to Apply

Simple steps to start your journey with BM Outsourcing

Application Process

We've made applying for our services as simple as possible. Follow these steps to get started with BM.

1

Submit the Form

ill out the application Google Form provided either on this website or through our official Facebook page. This is your first step in expressing your interest.

2

Wait for Email Confirmation

Keep an eye on your inbox! You’ll receive an email containing your interview schedule and next steps. Make sure to check spam/junk folders just in case.

3

Screening & Orientation

Attend the scheduled screening and account orientation session. This is an essential part of understanding the role and aligning with our expectations.

4

Mock Call

Participate in a mock call session to showcase your communication skills and readiness. This step helps assess your potential fit for the account.

5

Nesting Period

Undergo guided practice sessions known as nesting, where you’ll experience real scenarios with supervision to prepare for live work.

6

You're Hired!

Congratulations! Once you’ve completed all the steps, you’re officially hired and ready to start your journey with us.

Email Application

Please send your CV to bm.outsourcingmain@gmail.com with the following information:

Required Information

  • Full legal name
  • Current address
  • Contact information (phone and email)
  • Work experiences
  • Brief description of yourself
  • Referral (if any)
  • How you heard about BM

Response Timeline

We typically respond to all applications within 2 business days. If you haven't heard from us within this timeframe, please check your spam folder or contact us through alternative means.

Eligibility Information

Understanding who we serve and how we can best support your journey

Who We Welcome

We are proud to offer opportunities to a diverse range of individuals, including:

  • Working students
  • Work-from-home (WFH) freelancers
  • On-site employees
  • Career shifters and first-time job seekers

Whether you're looking to start your first job or transition into a new field, our program is designed to support you with the tools, training, and guidance needed to succeed.

Basic Requirements

To ensure a positive and productive experience for everyone, applicants should meet the following criteria:

  • Good communication skills and rapport
  • Conversational English proficiency
  • Must be 18 years old and above
  • Willingness to learn — no prior experience required

These simple yet essential requirements help us create an environment where growth and success are possible for all.
